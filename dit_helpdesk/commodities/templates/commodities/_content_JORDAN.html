The UK has signed a trade agreement with Jordan which is expected to come into effect on 1 May 2021.

This agreement largely maintains the previous EU trade agreement, including in most instances on duties. Until the agreement comes into effect, non-preferential rates may apply according to the UK’s MFN tariff schedule.

Jordan is also a beneficiary of the UK Generalised Scheme of Preferences (GSP) and the Enhanced Framework. Tariff preferences are available on selected product lines.

Jordan has exclusions on certain products. Please refer to the UK’s Generalised Scheme of Preferences.
