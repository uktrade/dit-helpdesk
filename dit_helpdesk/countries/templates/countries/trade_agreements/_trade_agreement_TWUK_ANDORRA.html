{{ country_name }} is part of a customs union with the EU.

The UK has agreed with the EU that all non-agricultural products (chapters 25-97) originating in {{ country_name }} are to be treated as originating in the EU. This is dependent on {{ country_name }} applying to UK products the same preferences that the EU applies to UK products.

Trade with {{ country_name }} of agricultural products (chapters 01-24) is not in scope of the UK-EU Trade and Cooperation Agreement and takes place under WTO terms.
