Opportunities

The UK government’s Contracts Finder lets you search for information about contracts worth over £10,000 with the UK government and its agencies.

The devolved nations of the UK (Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland) also run their own separate procurement portals:

  • Public Contracts Scotland
  • Sell2Wales
  • eSourcing NI
  • eTenders NI

At the end of the transition period a new service, ‘Find a Tender’ will be introduced. It will work in the same way as the EU’s Tender Electronic Daily (TED).

Existing portals such as, Contracts Finder, MOD Defence Contracts Online, Public Contracts Scotland, Sell2Wales and eTendersNI will be kept for location-specific procurement and lower threshold procurement.
