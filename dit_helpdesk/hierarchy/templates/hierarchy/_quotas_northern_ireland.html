{{ selected_origin_country_name }} United Kingdom

Quotas allow limited amounts of specific goods to be imported at a lower duty rate.

This product may be subject to either of the following:

  • UK quotas
  • EU quotas

Which quotas apply will depend on:

  • whether the product is intended for use in the UK or the EU
  • the proportions of each shipment intended for use in the UK and the EU respectively
  • whether UK-intended products may subsequently be imported into the Republic of Ireland

If you have a customs agent, freight forwarder or importer, they should be able to tell you which tariffs and charges apply to your shipment. Otherwise, please contact the Trader Support Service for more information.
