Change in tariff subheading (CTSH)

When a non-originating material is used to produce another finished product, the 6-digit level of the original commodity code will need to change once they have been incorporated into the final product. This is referred to as a change in tariff subheading.

Example:

Apple and mango juice comes under the commodity code 200990

A UK manufacturer makes apple and mango juice by combining imported mango juice and UK originating apple juice.

The 6-digit commodity code for the mango juice is 200989

The 6-digit commodity code for the mixed apple and mango juice is 200990

The mango juice has a change in commodity code at the 6-digit level. We call this a change in tariff subheading.
